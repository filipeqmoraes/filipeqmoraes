Filipe Moraes

Filmmaker

I made my first films when I was still in high school, I was 16 years old, when I received a VHS camcorder as a birthday present. In these 37 years of working in audiovisual, I have worked in all types of productions as a film maker or as a stage director, director of photography, screenwriter, editor and finisher. I am now dedicating myself to authorial documentary projects and learning programming.
